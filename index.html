The Dalahästs

We are an ultimate frisbee team. We value respect, diversity, support and growth. We have fun together.

We are a Sweden based team, with friends and players from all over the world. We are very focused on having fun, playing some good ultimate and extending the ever-growing circle of friends we have got going. Many would describe us as a pickup team, but we like more to think of ourselves as a community - a family.
